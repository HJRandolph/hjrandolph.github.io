Heather J. Randolph

During the week, I work at a telecommunications company where I used to research tariffs, set pricing, write training documentation, and explain complicated services in easy to understand terms. Now, I spend most of my time working with IT as a liaison for my team, writing SQL queries, developing reports, and improving processes through the magic of coding.

When I'm not at work, I am blogging or writing fiction. I also enjoy kickboxing, reading, playing the violin, and cooking for friends and family. I have been known to occasionally binge watch Sherlock or Doctor Who.
